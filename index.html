About Me

Hello, my name is Jennice! I am a fourth year computer engineering student at the University of Toronto. My university years have taken me on quite the journey! I have a passion for technology, people and biology which have all mixed quite well during my years at university. I did a little stint in the arts and science exploring the wonderous worlds of chemistry and neuroscience. Along the way I fell in love with coding and hopped back into engineering to pursue my software interests and haven't looked back since! Now I spend my time between hackathons, mental health advocacy and working on some personal projects. Here's some of my interesting stops along the way...

  • languageTour Guide

    I worked three summers as a tour guide for City Sightseeing Toronto giving tours atop bright red double decker buses imported from London England! I have done ~1000 hours worth of tours and have met some interesting folks along the way :)

  • whatshotDragonboater

    I had the pleasure of paddling with the Iron Dragons, the Universiy of Toronto Engineering Dragonboating Gold team for the 2016 season. Strong heart, Strong mind, Strong body!

  • starFlorist

    I worked as a florist for a boutique Floral&Decor store in Toronto. My job was a mix of roles, from managing facebook posts, online presence, creating video montages to arranging bridesmaid bouquets. It was a fun way to reach into my creative side!

  • assignmentResearch in the UK

    I did research at the University of Birmingham in the Tissue Regeneration and Interface Lab (TRAIL lab) where I looked into the development of scaffolds for osteochondral repairs. It was an amazing summer in the UK where I got a peek into the world of research and at the same time to explore different cities in England.

  • library_musicEvent Planner

    As part of several university clubs, I did a lot of event planning from organizing a conference to a Valentine's day party. For me it's a fun mix of organisation and chaos, all to bring people together.
