PartySmart Association, U of T

Web Content


PartySmart Association is a student group at the University of Toronto that actively encourages university students to party safely by collaborating with sponsors to provide food during events and free rides home. Other events hosted and participated include ‘Rethink the Drink’ conference, Toronto Frosh Festival 2013, Valentine’s Day events and parties within/outside the university community. I worked on the content and planning for the website.
